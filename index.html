Travel Safety

Travel smart, stay safe

Our editors have compiled essential safety information for every destination on this list.

🛡️

Travel Insurance

Never travel without comprehensive coverage. Ensure your policy includes medical evacuation, trip cancellation, and lost baggage. Compare policies on platforms like MoneySmart before you book.

💉

Health Precautions

Check with your GP 6–8 weeks before departure. Hep A, Typhoid, and Rabies vaccines are recommended for rural Southeast Asia. Carry a basic medical kit with antihistamines, rehydration salts, and diarrhoea medication.

🚨

Scam Awareness

Common scams in tourist areas include gem scams (Bangkok), taxi overcharging (Bali, Hanoi), and "closed temple" redirects. Always use metered taxis or ride-hailing apps. Book tours through your hotel.

Solo Travel Tips
